### Climbing Scales:

#### V Scale:

The V scale, aka the Hueco scale, is a rating system for bouldering difficulty, currently ranging from VB (easiest) to V17 (hardest). It originated in the late 1980s and early 1990s in Hueco Tanks, Texas, where American climber John “Vermin” Sherman developed the system to describe the difficulty of short, powerful boulder problems that were not well represented by traditional rope-climbing grades.
